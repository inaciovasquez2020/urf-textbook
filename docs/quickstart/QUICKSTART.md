# URF Quickstart Guide

## Purpose
Provide a short entry point for readers encountering the Unified Rigidity Framework.

## 5-Minute Overview

URF studies **limits of inference under locality and finite information capacity**.

The central mechanism:

Locality constraint  
→ bounded information transfer  
→ limited refinement power  
→ structural rigidity  
→ global obstruction.

## Key Concept

Local refinement algorithms cannot always reconstruct global structure.
URF explains when and why this failure is unavoidable.
