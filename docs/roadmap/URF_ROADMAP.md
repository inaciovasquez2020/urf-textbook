# Unified Rigidity Framework — Research Roadmap

## Purpose
Outline the staged development of the URF program and its remaining milestones.

## Stage I — Foundations
Status: Complete

Components:
- Definitions of locality and capacity constraints
- Rigidity / capacity invariant
- Initial structural lemmas

Repositories:
- urf-core
- urf-spine

## Stage II — Structural Mechanisms
Status: Largely complete

Focus:
- EntropyDepth formulation
- cycle-overlap rank instrumentation
- FO^k configuration analysis

Repository:
- chronos-urf-rr

## Stage III — Exposition and Integration
Status: Active

Goals:
- unified documentation
- worked examples
- dependency maps
- framework textbook

Repository:
- urf-textbook

## Stage IV — External Validation
Status: In progress

Activities:
- journal submissions
- referee feedback
- formal verification expansion
- additional empirical studies

## Stage V — Expansion
Future directions:

- spectral formulations of rigidity
- extensions to dynamical systems
- broader complexity-theoretic applications

## Long-Term Objective

Develop a unified explanation for structural limits of inference and
refinement processes under locality and finite information capacity.
