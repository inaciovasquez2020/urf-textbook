# URF Papers — Summary of Results

## Purpose
Provide a concise overview of the main research results associated with
the Unified Rigidity Framework.

## Core Result Types

### Rigidity Mechanisms
Structural limits arising from locality and bounded information capacity.

### EntropyDepth Bounds
Lower bounds on the number of refinement steps required to resolve
structural ambiguity.

### Cycle–Local Rigidity
Connections between cycle complexity and diversity of local logical types.

### Capacity Constraints
Information-theoretic limits on refinement and inference processes.

## Example Application Areas

- graph structure reconstruction
- limits of local algorithms
- logical definability in finite structures
- dynamical rigidity phenomena

## Current Research Phase

Active journal submissions and external validation of the framework.
