# IECP Local Branching Lemma Sketch

## Target Lemma
Find constants
\[
r_0,\tau,\kappa,c_0,C_0,\varepsilon_0>0
\]
such that
\[
\int_t^{t+\tau}\|\nabla \Phi_s(u_0)\|_{L^\infty}\,ds \ge \log\kappa
\Longrightarrow
N_{\varepsilon_0}\!\big(\mathcal R(t+\tau;u_0)\big)\ge e^{c_0} N_{\varepsilon_0}\!\big(\mathcal R(t;u_0)\big).
\]

## Proof Skeleton
1. Local stretching produces \(M\ge e^{c_0}\) separated descendant supports.
2. Equal-size admissible perturbations on those supports remain dynamically distinguishable at time \(\tau\).
3. Distinguishability yields an \(\varepsilon_0\)-separated family in phase space.
4. Hence covering number multiplies by at least \(e^{c_0}\).
