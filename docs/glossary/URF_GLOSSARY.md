# Unified Rigidity Framework — Glossary

## Purpose
Provide a quick reference for core terminology used across the URF ecosystem.

## Terms

### Locality
A restriction that information or computation can only access bounded-radius
neighborhoods of a structure.

### Capacity
A bound on the amount of information that can be transferred or refined
during an inference or reconstruction process.

### Rigidity
A structural condition where local constraints force global limitations
on reconstruction or inference.

### FO^k Local Type
The first-order logical type of a vertex determined by its radius-R
neighborhood under k-variable logic.

### Cycle-Overlap Rank (COR)
A structural measure capturing the complexity of overlapping cycles in
bounded-radius neighborhoods of a graph.

### EntropyDepth
A lower bound measuring the number of refinement steps required to resolve
structural ambiguity in a local inference system.

### Refinement Process
An iterative method that gradually distinguishes configurations using
locally available information.

### Structural Obstruction
A mechanism that prevents a refinement or inference process from achieving
global reconstruction under locality constraints.

## Usage
These terms appear throughout the URF repositories and manuscripts.
This glossary provides consistent definitions for readers and referees.
