\begin{theorem}[Terminal Obstruction Template]
\textbf{Dependencies: } D6, L4
\textbf{Status: } Proven

Let $L$ be a self-adjoint operator on Hilbert space $\mathcal H$ and let $V \subset \mathcal H$ be a finite-dimensional defect subspace.

If
\[
\lambda_1(L|_{V^\perp}) > 0,
\]
then all non-defect modes are uniformly coercive:
\[
\langle \psi, L \psi \rangle \ge \lambda_1 \|\psi\|^2
\quad \text{for all } \psi \in V^\perp.
\]

Consequently, any structural reduction whose only obstruction lies in $V$ reduces to verifying positivity on $V^\perp$.
\end{theorem}
