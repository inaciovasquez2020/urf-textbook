\begin{lemma}[Capacity Bound]
\textbf{Dependencies: } D1, D2
\textbf{Status: } Proven

Let a refinement step produce transcript $T$ with capacity bound $C$.
Then the mutual information satisfies
\[
I(X;T) \leq C.
\]

Equivalently, any admissible refinement must satisfy
\[
\mathrm{EntropyReduction} \leq C.
\]

In particular, no single refinement step can exceed its transcript capacity.
\end{lemma}
