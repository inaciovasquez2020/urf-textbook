\chapter*{Preface}
\addcontentsline{toc}{chapter}{Preface}

This book develops the Unified Rigidity Framework (URF),
a structural theory of computation and physical systems
based on bounded information capacity.

The central thesis is:

\[
\text{Global behavior is constrained by local capacity bounds.}
\]

Across logic, algorithms, spectral theory,
gauge theory, and fluid dynamics,
we demonstrate that stabilization, rigidity,
and depth lower bounds arise from
non-amplification of information.

The framework is invariant-driven,
capacity-based,
and normalization-complete.
