\section{Locality of Refinement Processes}

Let \(R\) denote the locality radius.

\begin{definition}
A refinement step is \(R\)-local if the update at vertex \(v\) depends only on the radius-\(R\) neighborhood of \(v\).
\end{definition}

\begin{theorem}[Locality Constraint]
If refinement is \(R\)-local and the system capacity is bounded, global entropy reduction per step is bounded.
\end{theorem}
