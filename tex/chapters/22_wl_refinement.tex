\chapter{Weisfeiler--Lehman Refinement}

\section{WL Update}

For graph \(G=(V,E)\),

\[
c_{t+1}(v) = hash(c_t(v), multiset\{c_t(u): u \in N(v)\})
\]

\section{Stabilization}

The refinement stabilizes after at most \(n\) iterations.

\section{Relation to EntropyDepth}

WL refinement performs bounded entropy reduction per iteration.
