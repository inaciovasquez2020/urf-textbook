\chapter{Information Capacity Limits}

\section{System Capacity}

Let \(C\) denote the information generation capacity of a system.

\[
I_t \le C
\]

for every refinement step \(t\).

\section{Capacity Constraint}

\begin{theorem}
No refinement system can generate more information per step than its capacity.
\end{theorem}

\section{Implication}

Entropy reduction across a computation is bounded by cumulative capacity.
