\chapter{Complexity Implications}

\section{Refinement Complexity}

If entropy decreases by at most \(c\) per step,

\[
D \ge \frac{H_0}{c}.
\]

\section{Implication for Search Problems}

For configuration space size \(2^n\),

\[
H_0 = n.
\]

Thus

\[
D = \Omega(n).
\]

\section{Rigidity Barrier}

Local refinement algorithms cannot collapse entropy faster than permitted by capacity bounds.
