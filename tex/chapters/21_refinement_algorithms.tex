\chapter{Refinement Algorithms}

\section{Partition Refinement}

Let \(P_t\) be a partition of the configuration space.

\[
P_{t+1} = R(P_t)
\]

where \(R\) refines blocks according to local invariants.

\section{Local Refinement Rule}

For vertex \(v\),

\[
label_{t+1}(v) = f(label_t(N_R(v)))
\]

where \(N_R(v)\) denotes the radius-\(R\) neighborhood.

\section{Termination}

The process terminates when

\[
P_{t+1} = P_t.
\]
