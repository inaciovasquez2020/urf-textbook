\section*{Glossary}

\textbf{Refinement Process:} A sequence of local updates reducing admissible configuration space.

\textbf{Operational Rigidity:} Collapse of refinement when entropy reduction exceeds transcript capacity.

\textbf{Terminal Obstruction:} A final inequality or coercivity condition preventing further compression.

\textbf{Entropy Depth (ED):} Minimum sequential refinement depth required to resolve entropy $H$ under bounded capacity.
